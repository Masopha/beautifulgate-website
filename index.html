Who We Are

A Family, Not Just
an Organization

"Assisting vulnerable children (0–5 yrs) in Lesotho to confidently embrace their future, walking with Christ, living with hope, now and for eternity."

Founded in 2001 in Ha Thetsane Lesia, Maseru. In a country where 2 in 5 people survive on less than $1 a day and 1 in 4 are HIV positive, children are painfully vulnerable. We exist to give them their childhood back — and find them forever families.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Vision

Every decision we make, every child we hold, is anchored in a purpose greater than ourselves.

🎯

Our Mission

To restore dignity, hope, and opportunity to vulnerable children and families through holistic care, education, and development — rooted in Christian faith and driven by love.

🌟

Our Vision

Thriving communities where every child is valued, protected, educated, and given the opportunity to reach their full God-given potential — in a forever family.
